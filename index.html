Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do plant types and sensor sensitivity influence the necessary soil moisture percentage in humid and drought conditions?

    Different types of plants exhibit varying needs for soil moisture, which are detected by the sensor. The device determines the necessity of adding water based on this percentage, ensuring optimal irrigation operations.

  • What materials are required for the automatic irrigation system?

    Arduino Uno
    Soil moisture sensor
    DC water pump
    Relay
    Breadboard
    Battery
    Jumper wires
    Plants
    Water bottle
    Enclosure box

  • What are the variables in the study?

    Independent variables: Different types of plants, changes in soil moisture detected by the sensor.
    Dependent variable: Decision to irrigate based on soil moisture percentage.
    Control variables: Consistent environmental conditions, standard calibration of the sensor.

  • What is the aim of modern automatic irrigation?

    The aim is to optimize water usage in soil irrigation by measuring soil moisture and conducting irrigation when the soil reaches a certain dryness threshold. This process prevents soil damage and ensures efficient water management.

  • How are specific moisture levels determined for irrigation?

    Specific percentages of soil moisture are determined based on the needs of various plant types. The project objectives focus on saving time and effort, maintaining optimal plant conditions, and providing quick and safe watering, ultimately contributing to landscape maintenance and soil revegetation.
